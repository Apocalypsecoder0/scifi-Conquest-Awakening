Introduction

Welcome to the Sci-Fi Conquest Game, a text-based turn-based strategy game where players can explore the universe, manage resources, build fleets, and engage in interstellar combat. This project is inspired by classic RTS MMORPGs and aims to provide a simplified, yet engaging experience.

In this game, you will:

  • Explore new planets and galaxies
  • Manage and optimize your resources
  • Build and upgrade your fleets
  • Engage in strategic interstellar combat
  • Form alliances and compete with other players

Prepare yourself for an adventure across the stars!
