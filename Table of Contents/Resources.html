Resources

In the Sci-Fi Conquest Game, managing resources efficiently is crucial for building and upgrading your fleets. The main resources in the game include:

Minerals

Minerals are the primary building blocks for constructing ships and structures. They can be mined from planets and asteroids.

Energy

Energy is required to power your fleets and structures. It can be generated from various sources, including solar panels and fusion reactors.

Technology

Technology points are used to research new advancements and upgrades for your fleets and structures. They can be earned through exploration and scientific endeavors.

Credits

Credits are the game's currency, used for trading and purchasing resources and upgrades. They can be earned through missions and trading routes.
