Gameplay

The Sci-Fi Conquest Game is a text-based turn-based strategy game where players can explore the universe, manage resources, build fleets, and engage in interstellar combat.

Exploration

Discover new planets and galaxies as you navigate through the universe. Each new location offers unique resources and challenges.

Resource Management

Collect and manage resources efficiently to build and upgrade your fleets. Resources include minerals, energy, and technology.

Fleet Building

Construct and upgrade a variety of ships to form powerful fleets. Each ship type has distinct strengths and weaknesses.

Interstellar Combat

Engage in strategic battles with other players or AI-controlled enemies. Use your fleet's capabilities to outmaneuver and defeat opponents.

Alliances

Form alliances with other players to strengthen your position and collaborate on large-scale objectives.
