FAQ - RTS Turn-Based MMORPG

Gameplay

What is this game about?

This is a turn-based, real-time strategy (RTS) MMORPG where you build your empire, manage resources, and conquer opponents in a futuristic space setting.

Is the game turn-based or real-time?

It combines turn-based mechanics for tactical decisions with real-time elements for long-term strategy management.

How do I earn resources?

Resources can be earned by mining planets, trading with other players, or completing missions and battles.

Accounts

Is the game free to play?

Yes, the game is free to play with optional in-game purchases for premium features.

How can I reset my account?

You can reset your account from the account settings page. Please note this action is irreversible.

Can I have multiple accounts?

Each player is allowed one account per server. Violating this rule may result in account suspension.

Combat

How do battles work?

Battles are turn-based. You select your fleet, issue commands, and resolve conflicts during each turn.

Can I join alliances for combat?

Yes! Joining alliances allows you to participate in large-scale wars and gain access to shared resources and strategies.

What happens if I lose a battle?

You'll lose some resources and ships, but you can rebuild your fleet over time. Alliances may also support you in rebuilding.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

The game works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported.

How can I report a bug?

You can report bugs through the in-game support system or by emailing support@example.com.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes! The game is optimized for mobile devices and can be played through a browser or our official app.
